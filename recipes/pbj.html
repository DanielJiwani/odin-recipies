PB&J Recipe

The complete product!

Ingredients List:

  • Bread of choice
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly/Jam of choice
  • Milk

Steps:

  • Take out 2 slices of bread. Toast if you'd like.
  • Spread peanut butter on one piece of the bread and spread the jelly/jam on the other
  • Join the 2 slices so the peanute butter and jelly/jam are touching and enjoy with some milk!
