Ramen Recipe

The complete product!

Ingredients List:

  • Instant Ramen Packet
  • Water

Steps:

  • Bring water to a boil.
  • Add the noodles from the instant ramen packed. Set the seasonings aside.
  • Once noodles are cooked pour whatever water you don't want.
  • Add your seasoning packet that came with the instat ramen. Mix and Enjoy!
