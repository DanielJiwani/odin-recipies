Lasagna Recipe

The complete product!

Ingredients List:

  • Pasta
  • Choice of Meat
  • Sauce
  • Oregano

Steps:

  • Boil water, add salt, add pasta, cook until audente, set aside 4oz of pasta water
  • Cook meat with seasonings of your choice.
  • Pour sauce into pan with cooked meat and let it become warm.
  • Put the cooked pasta into the pan with the sauce and meat and add pasta water to get the thickness you like.
  • Eat and enjoy!
